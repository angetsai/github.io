Hey everyone! My name is Angel and I'm a senior in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. I am on the User Experience path and because I'm interested in design and how people respond and connect with interfaces. I am also part of Dance Marathon at the University of Michigan, the largest student org on campus that raises funds and awareness for pediatric rehabilitation therapies at C.S Mott Children's Hospital and Beaumont Children's. I believe in children and their strength, their joy, and their childhoods. Above are some photos from activities I had the opportunity to experience because of DMUM -- Mott's Summer Enrichment Camp and VictorThon.


In addition to volunteering, I am also one of the Information Management Chairs for DMUM, along with Colleen Feola. Our team also includes three coordinators, and together, we maintain the official website and the database of Dancer information. We also like fun projects, like working with an outside developer to create a mobile application to be used in-event. #InfoManaged
